Nipples tips
Nipple types

Nipple types: can be flat, protruding, inverted or unclassified (multiple or divided), it is possible to have one breast with one type from the other breast in the same individual.

Inverted nipples

Inverted nipples, are sunk in, instead of protruding out, the function the same as the protruded ones and may be sunken all the time or come in and out. Breast-feeding often brings them out.

Breast-feeding mother

Nipple pain for a breast-feeding mother may be a sign or a result of poor positioning during breast-feeding. For women not breast-feeding, nipple pain may be related to your monthly period cycle, hormone changes, skin irritation, skin allergies or friction from a bra as may happen with sports bras.

Bumps on the areola

Bumps on the areola are called Montgomery glands they serve to lubricate the nipple and they are a normal part of the anatomy.
